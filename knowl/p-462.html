The nearest neighbours are: Jean-Louis and Josianne 1km away down by the A21, the main road leading to the Pyrénées; and also 1km away in the other direction, the village idiot and his brother and sister-in-law further inland. Only the postman would drive up to the house. Or the neighbours, or friends and relatives, maybe the occasional first-time visitor — to buy wood from Serge’s little sawmill.
