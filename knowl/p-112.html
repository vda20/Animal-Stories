I’d ignore him, avoiding eye contact. He’d start licking his lips: tongue swish one way. I’d ignore him. Tongue swish the other direction. I’d ignore him. Swish one way, then the other in rapid succession. I’d ignore him. Swish one way, then the other, then swallow visibly hard and audibly loud — GULP! Sometimes more than once to drive his message home. Only then would I part with a piece of my fruit.
