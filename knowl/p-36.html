When the pond was drained, I’d lean over the side of the enclosure wall, use a long-handled broom to chase the crocs off the dry-land sections down into the emptied pond in the middle, scrub the floor of the dry-land areas, and hose down. Then, I’d use the broom to chase the crocodiles back on to the dry-land sections, so that I could scrub and rinse out the pond. Once this was done, I’d put the bung back in (on the outside!), and turn on the tap inside, to fill the pond.
