As an Alsatian, it was in Bear Bear’s blood to be fierce and fearsome. (Almost 60 years on, it’s suddenly struck me that the sound “boo”, in the S.E. Chinese dialects of 潮州 / Cháozhōu / Teochew and 福建 / Fújiàn / Hokkien, can be 武 (wǔ in Mandarin), meaning “military, valiant, fierce”. It is the same wǔ as in wǔshù 武術 / martial arts. So it was a very appropriate name after all, serendipitously.)
