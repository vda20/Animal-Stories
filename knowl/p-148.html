After each beating, I would go and sit with him, still chained to the pillar, putting my arms around his neck, crying and stroking him to comfort him and ease some of the physical and emotional pain and humiliation of being beaten and shouted at. “Why do you keep doing this? Don’t you know that you’re the one to suffer in the end? Why haven’t you learned after all these beatings? Don’t do it again. For your own sake.”
