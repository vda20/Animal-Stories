Jeanette’s husband Serge had left some bales of hay in the barn/garage area for temporary storage. The hen took to laying an egg on top of one of the bales. After Serge removed the bales, she carried on laying her eggs there, straight onto the concrete floor, in the exact same spot where she’d been laying them thus far on the top of the bale. Habits do die hard even with hens, it seems.
