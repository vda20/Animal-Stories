The enclosure for the adults was a 15’x5’ concrete oblong, surrounded by brick walls about 3’ high. It looks like one of those contraptions placed over a bath, for holding the soap, shampoo and back scrubber — called a bath (caddy) rack: dipping in the middle (for the wet-pond section), rising up gently to level areas at each end (for the dry-land sections).
