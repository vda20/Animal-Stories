What can you do when it’s someone else’s place? The point of my presence, anyway, as in the case of staying with Jiang (see Jiang the Afghan cat), is to keep the cat company: help it feel less alone and deserted. So, I sat in an armchair, reading, with my feet up on a pouffe. Xiǎo Lóng went on my lap: on all fours, with his face towards me but head dropped, in a doze.
