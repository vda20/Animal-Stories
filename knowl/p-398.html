Right from the start, Dino failed to fit in, by rubbing the other dogs up the wrong way — more specifically, up the wrong end by sniffing their underparts. They took umbrage at this newcomer taking such an over-familiar approach, and warned him off with a few growls at the back of the throat and some rising hackles. Which should’ve been clear to Dino, except that he doesn’t seem to read body language messages too well, and went ahead with his never-say-die way of doing things.
