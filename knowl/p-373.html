(Haha, even the dogs have Chinese habits! It’s common for the Chinese to have 宵夜 xiāo yè / late night snack, perhaps because they usually eat dinner around 6:30 / 7 pm, so they’d be hungry by 11 pm — if they’re still up. Where I grew up in a suburb in Singapore, we could go out at that late hour and get fried or soup noodles from some roadside stall to eat after a mahjong session.)
