For some reason, Big Dog went for a brick wall with gaps in it rather than a solid one — the bricks were staggered at each level, giving a chequerboard effect. He should be rich enough not to have to worry about paying for a few more bricks, surely [meow]. He was obviously even more unsociable than the previous occupants — the eccentric mother-and-son team, building a wall that made sure we couldn’t look into their garden and house. Like as if we had nothing better to do.
